HP Linux Printing Support

As a convenience to our customers who are installing Linux, HP has provided driver solutions for printers supported by Linux.

The HP driver project provides printing support for nearly 1,330printer models, including Deskjet, Officejet, Photosmart, PSC, Business Inkjet and LaserJet. (Note: not all models are currently supported. See Supported Devices for more information.)

This Website provides support for these solutions. In addition to the information within this site, the HPLIP Launchpad.net Page is available for support questions and to contact the HPLIP team.

Phone support is currently not available for HPLIP.
